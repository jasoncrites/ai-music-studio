## Mobile-Specific User Needs

### Touch Interface
- Touch-native apps hold highest market share and fastest growth
- Users expect: swipe-based editing, pinch-to-zoom, thumb-optimized layouts
- Popular apps: Koala Sampler, Roland Zenbeats, Cubasis 3, Fender Studio (free, launched May 2025)

### Recording Quality
- High-end expectation: UHQ 32-bit, Dolby Atmos support
- External mics preferred over internal (condenser mics ~$100)
- Audio interfaces: Focusrite Scarlett Solo + Audio-Technica 2020 standard budget setup

### Battery Performance
- Users expect full-day to multi-day battery life
- OnePlus 15: 3-day battery life (current leader)
- External wireless mics: 5+ hours minimum for uninterrupted recording

### Platform Preferences
- **iOS:** High-end production (iPad Pro M-series chips, Apple Pencil integration)
- **Android:** Fastest growth (affordability, wider reach, improving audio frameworks)

## Country Music Production Insights

### Nashville Industry Trends
- **Pro Tools HD2** remains Nashville commercial standard
- **AI adoption ubiquitous:** "From entry-level songwriters to top dogs"
- **AI use case:** Primarily demo production (lyrics, backup vocals, melodies via Suno)
- **Industry concern:** Authenticity vs. efficiency tension
- **Market shift:** East/West coast labels expanding into Nashville, indie artists bypassing traditional system

### Country Production Characteristics
- **Core element:** Acoustic guitars at heart of country songs
- **Compression:** LA-3A and Fairchild compressors standard
- **Effects:** Classic EQ + big room reverb
- **Recording philosophy:** Live guitar + vocals (no overdubs) for authentic feel
- **Mic technique:** Point at 12th fret (never sound hole), condenser cardioid pattern

### Acoustic Guitar/Vocal Recording
- **Budget mobile setup:** Smartphone + free DAW ($0)
- **Entry-level setup:** Focusrite Scarlett Solo + AT2020 mic (<$300)
- **Professional mobile setup:** iPad Pro + audio interface + condenser mics
- **Room treatment:** Carpet, curtains, soft furnishings to absorb reverb
- **Free DAWs:** Ardour, Audacity, Presonus Free, Reaper ($39-60)
